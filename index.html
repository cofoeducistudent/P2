  • I am a full-stack software developer creating websites and bespoke software. These range from small, static yet responsive websites to more complex full stack projects.

    What sets me apart from my peers is that I am technically trained and extensively experienced within the information technology sphere.

    Having coded since my teens I qualified in BTEC Level 3 micro-electronics, initially landing a job as a circuit board assembler and repair and systems wiring technician. I then moved into the field service engineering as an EPOS Sales & Solutions Architect. I progressed into computer field service-repair, then network support. Third line resolution support and build engineering were my next steps, which led me to project engineering, ultimately advancing to full-lifecycle, end-to-end technical project management.

    Having always loved computing from the 8-bit micro era, where I cut my teeth in assembler coding and games software writing, I decided to augment my career and move into software development, qualifying with a Software Development Diploma.

    What does all this mean for you? Simply put, when I work with you, I bring all my empirical knowledge woven in the fabric of IT with me and into your projects.
