  • Clement Ofoedu Software Developer

  • Hi, I am a full-stack software developer.

    I create websites and bespoke software, ranging from small, static yet responsive to more complex full stack web sites.However, what sets me apart from my peers is that I am technically trained within the information technology sphere and not just by reading books. I was an amateur electronics hobbyist and micro-coder in my youth. Information technology was a natural progression for me.

    I pursued electronics and coding from my teens, landing a job initially as a factory circuit board assembler, repairer, and systems wiring technician. I then moved into field service engineering, progressing to an EPOS sales & solutions Architect.I moved into computer field service-repair, then network support. Third line resolution support and build engineering were my next steps, which led me to project engineering, ultimately advancing to full-lifecycle, end-to-end technical project management.

    Having always loved computing from the 8bit micro era, where I cut my teeth in assembler coding and games writing software, I decided to augment my career and move into software development. What does all this mean? Simply put, when I work with you, I bring all my empirical knowledge woven in the fabric of IT to bear.
